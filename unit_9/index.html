Hey! My name is Addie. I am a junior at Simmons University in Boston, MA. I have lived in New England for most of my life, other than the brief time when I went to Flagler College in St. Augustine, FL. I love minimalistic art, rock music, and I was raised by two hippie parents. I am a go-with-the-flow kind of girl and I always follow my heart over my head. As long as I can remember I have loved art – whether it was finger painting, drawing or some sort of mixed media, my life has been surrounded by art.
